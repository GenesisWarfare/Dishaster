## 1. Market & Trends Overview  

- **Global market size (2024)**: The global video games market is forecast to generate **$187.7 billion** in 2024, marking a +2.1% year-on-year growth. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}  
- **Breakdown by platform**:  
  - PC revenues: ~$43.2 billion, +4.0% YoY. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}  
  - Mobile gaming (depending on source) estimated at ~$139.4 billion in 2024, with projected growth to ~$157.6 billion in 2025 (CAGR ~10.2% from 2025-2030). :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}  
- **Genre & platform trends**:  
  - Mobile: After a decline in 2022-2023, signs of recovery in spend and downloads in 2024. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}  
  - Single-player, casual or mid-core experiences (i.e., non-competitive, story/arcade/management) are increasingly favoured in PC/mobile platforms. For example, PC growth driven by “evergreen” and cross-platform titles. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}  
- **Relevance to your concept (Dishaster)**:  
  - Your game blends management/serving + comedic chaos + stealth/party elements — a mid-core casual experience suitable for mobile *and* PC.  
  - The trend toward shorter session, accessible games with humor and replayability fits well with the market direction.  
  - Also, “party-style” or casual chaos games (easy to pick up, fun loops, strong replay value) align with what players are currently more open to.  
- **Growth outlook**:  
  - Overall market growth is modest (single-digit %). :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}  
  - Mobile remains largest segment and has the strongest growth potential long term.  
  - Management/chaos casual genres may not be the top-growth segment (compared to live-service/social/multiplayer), but they benefit from lower competition and high accessibility.  
- **Key trends impacting the genre**:  
  - Hybrid-casual design (mixing easy mechanics + bite-sized sessions)  
  - Replayability with varied environments/events (important for your banquet/venue variety)  
  - Monetisation via upgrades/cosmetics rather than hard pay-walls (fits tip/upgrades model)  
  - Cross-platform play & local multiplayer/co-op (your optional co-op/versus waiter mode)
